Rawaz – Ramp Walk Extravaganza

Timing: 5:00 PM – 8:00 PM

Rawaz is the glamorous ramp walk show of Pravah Fest. It’s a celebration of fashion, confidence, and creativity where students showcase their style and charisma on stage.

  • Fashion themes and creative outfits
  • Spotlight moments for participants
  • Titles for Best Walk, Best Style, Best Personality

Treasure Hunt

Timing: 10:00 AM – 1:00 PM

Treasure Hunt is an adventurous race across the campus! Teams decode riddles, solve puzzles, and follow hidden clues to reach the final treasure.

  • Brain‑teasing riddles and challenges
  • Explore hidden corners of the campus
  • Test teamwork, speed, and wit
  • Exciting prizes for winners

Murder Mystery

Timing: 2:00 PM – 5:00 PM

Step into the world of suspense and intrigue! Work with your team to analyze clues, interrogate suspects, and solve the thrilling murder mystery before time runs out.

  • Immersive storytelling and roleplay
  • Puzzle solving and clue hunting
  • Compete against other teams
